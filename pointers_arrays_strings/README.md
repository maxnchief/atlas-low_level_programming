If you are a fan of non-linear exploration, space travel, and solving mysteries then you'd love the game Outer Wilds. It is the type of game to go in best blindly and has become one of my favorite games of all time. It is an incredible unique experience that no game has come close to replicating. Not to be confused with the Obisidan RPG Outer Worlds because that happens a bit. I don't want to go too much into detail becase it's best to find out for yourself but something I will share is Outer Wilds is played in a small scale solar system with several planets orbiting a star. You are an astronaut who can freely explore this star system. Every inch of every planet is explorable as everything is simmiltaniously loaded, meaning there is no fast travel or loading screens to go from one planet to another. Thats only part of what makes the game special so I urge you to play it. Don't watch or read anything further to avoid spoilers. It's a tremendious experience and its like only $20 or something like that.
